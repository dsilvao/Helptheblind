About Help The Blind

We Support Visually Impaired Students Achieve Their Dreams Through University Education.

TRAINING PROGRAMs

E-MET program. A 2 year program with goal of skill training in Computers, English, Mobility, Analytical abilities, and Interpersonal relationships

SPONSORSHIPs

We have developed a sponsoship program to provide financial support for college bound visually impaired students pursuing graduate course from reputed colleges in integrated education system

ASSISTIVE DEVICES SUPPORT.

We have provided Laptops to our top 16 student-beneficiaries and to 5 colleges. We also provide a recording device knwn as the Plex Talk to students to better their reading.
